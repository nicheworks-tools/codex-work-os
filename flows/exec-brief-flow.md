# Executive Brief Flow

## Purpose
Prepare short high-signal briefs for leaders before or after meetings.

## Use when
- A leader needs a fast overview
- You need pre-meeting context
- You need post-meeting follow-up in a concise format

## Inputs
- Meeting context
- Notes, emails, or thread summaries
- Time-sensitive issues
- Stakeholder names and open decisions

## Process
1. Clarify what the executive needs to know now.
2. Remove low-signal detail.
3. Highlight material risks, deadlines, and unresolved decisions.
4. Prepare a concise recommendation.
5. Add a ready-to-forward summary if needed.

## Required output
1. Why this matters
2. Current situation
3. Key risks / sensitivities
4. Decisions or actions needed
5. Optional forwardable draft

## Quality bar
- Short, decisive, high signal
- No unnecessary background
- Easy to skim in under two minutes
