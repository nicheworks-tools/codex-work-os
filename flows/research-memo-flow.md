# Research Memo Flow

## Purpose
Turn raw notes and sources into a clear comparison or decision memo.

## Use when
- Comparing options
- Summarizing findings from multiple sources
- Preparing a recommendation with evidence

## Inputs
- Research notes
- Candidate options or competitors
- Constraints and decision criteria

## Process
1. Define the question being answered.
2. List the sources and what each contributes.
3. Compare options using explicit criteria.
4. Separate evidence from inference.
5. Recommend a next step and state uncertainty.

## Required output
1. Question
2. Evidence summary
3. Comparison
4. Risks / gaps
5. Recommendation
6. Confidence / uncertainty

## Quality bar
- No fake certainty
- Traceable reasoning
- A reader should understand both the conclusion and its limits
