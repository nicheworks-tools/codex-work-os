# PM Brief Flow

## Purpose
Convert scattered project updates into a decision-friendly PM brief.

## Use when
- Status updates are spread across notes, Slack, or docs
- A weekly or ad hoc progress brief is needed
- Stakeholders need a quick situation snapshot

## Inputs
- Progress notes
- Risks, blockers, dependencies
- Current goals and time constraints

## Process
1. Define the reporting window and goal.
2. Separate completed work from ongoing work.
3. Identify blockers, dependencies, and missing decisions.
4. Highlight the most important risks.
5. Recommend the next actions in priority order.

## Required output
1. Goal / reporting scope
2. Current status
3. Progress made
4. Risks / blockers
5. Decisions needed
6. Recommended next actions

## Quality bar
- Facts first, interpretation second
- No vanity reporting
- Make the brief usable by someone who did not follow the thread
