# Sales Response Flow

## Purpose
Turn messy inbound notes, Slack threads, or email fragments into a clean sales response package.

## Use when
- A lead or prospect message is unclear or scattered
- You need a reply draft quickly
- You need to separate facts, assumptions, and follow-up questions

## Inputs
- Prospect message or thread
- Internal notes
- Offer, constraints, and next-step preferences

## Process
1. Identify the actual request, urgency, and commercial intent.
2. Extract confirmed facts only.
3. List missing information that blocks a useful reply.
4. Propose the best next step.
5. Draft a response that is clear, polite, and easy to send.

## Required output
1. Goal
2. Confirmed facts
3. Open questions
4. Recommended next step
5. Ready-to-send draft

## Quality bar
- No invented pricing or commitments
- No overpromising
- Keep the draft short enough to actually send
