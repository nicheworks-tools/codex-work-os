# Sales Tone Guide

## Core rules
- Be clear before being persuasive.
- Never sound uncertain about confirmed facts.
- Never sound certain about unconfirmed facts.
- Make the next step easy to understand.
- Keep the draft concise enough to actually send.

## Strong sales reply signals
- clear acknowledgment of the request
- visible next step
- polite confidence
- no pressure-heavy language
- no fake certainty

## Weak sales reply patterns
- vague enthusiasm without substance
- hidden assumptions
- overlong explanations
- invented scope or pricing
- passive ending with no CTA

## Compression rule
If the source material is messy, compress toward:
1. what they need
2. what is confirmed
3. what is still unclear
4. what should happen next
