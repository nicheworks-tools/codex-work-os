# Example Sales Reply

## Goal
Respond to an inbound prospect asking about a possible internal pilot and next-step discussion.

## Confirmed facts
- The prospect wants to explore a pilot.
- They appear to want a lightweight initial rollout.
- They asked about timing and what would be needed to get started.

## Open questions
- How many users are expected in the pilot?
- What internal systems would need to be included?
- Is there a fixed internal deadline for launch?

## Recommended next step
Reply with a short confirmation, ask the minimum missing questions, and propose a short discovery call.

## Ready-to-send draft
Thanks for reaching out. A lightweight pilot sounds feasible, and a small initial rollout is usually the fastest way to get started.

To make sure we recommend the right setup, could you share a bit more about the expected number of users, any key systems that would need to be included, and whether you have a target timeline in mind?

Once we have that context, we can suggest the best next step and outline what a pilot would likely involve. If helpful, we can also set up a short call to walk through it together.
