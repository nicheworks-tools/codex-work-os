# Meeting Pack Template

## Meeting objective
What this meeting is supposed to accomplish.

## Current situation
What the leader should know before entering the meeting.

## Key stakeholders
- name
- role
- relevance

## Risks / sensitivities
- issue
- why it matters
- possible effect on discussion

## Decisions to prepare for
- decision
- likely trade-off
- recommended stance

## Follow-up hooks
- likely next step
- likely owner
- note for post-meeting follow-up
