# Executive Assist Pack

## Purpose
Turn noisy context into short, high-signal executive support outputs.

## Use this pack when
- preparing a leader for a meeting
- summarizing a situation for a quick executive read
- turning notes into a follow-up brief
- clarifying priorities, risks, and decisions
- producing short forwardable summaries

## Do not use this pack when
- the task is mainly a PM weekly status brief
- the task is a research comparison memo
- the request is a sales reply
- the task is a customer support escalation
- the main need is implementation rather than briefing

## Inputs
- meeting notes, email fragments, Slack threads, or summary scraps
- audience if known
- current priorities, deadlines, or risks
- open decisions and owners if known

## Required behavior
1. Optimize for signal density.
2. Remove nonessential detail.
3. Surface time-sensitive issues early.
4. Separate current reality from assumptions.
5. End with clear decisions, actions, or a forwardable draft when useful.

## Default output order
1. Why this matters
2. Current situation
3. Key risks / sensitivities
4. Decisions or actions needed
5. Optional forwardable draft

## Quality standard
A leader should be able to skim the output quickly and still understand:
- why this matters now
- what the situation is
- what the risks are
- what needs a decision or follow-up
