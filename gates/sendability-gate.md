# Sendability Gate

Use this gate before finalizing any outward-facing draft.

## Pass conditions
- The draft is polite and clear
- The ask or next step is obvious
- No unconfirmed claims are presented as facts
- Tone matches professional business communication
- The message is short enough to send without rewriting

## Fail conditions
- Vague request or no clear CTA
- Hidden assumptions
- Overly defensive or robotic tone
- Too long for the likely channel
- Missing critical context

## Minimum fix rule
If the draft fails, shorten first, then clarify, then soften tone.
