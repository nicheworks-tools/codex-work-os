# Brief Signal Gate

Use this gate before finalizing any brief intended for a busy decision-maker.

## Pass conditions
- The brief explains why it matters now
- The current situation is clear in a few lines
- The most important risk is visible
- A decision or action is clearly stated
- Low-signal detail is removed

## Fail conditions
- Too much background before relevance
- No visible action or decision point
- Risk is buried
- The brief assumes too much prior context
- The output is long but not decisive

## Minimum fix rule
Rewrite in this order:
1. Why this matters
2. Current situation
3. Key risk
4. Action or decision needed
