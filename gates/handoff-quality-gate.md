# Handoff Quality Gate

Use this gate before finalizing any escalation note or ownership handoff.

## Pass conditions
- The problem is stated clearly
- Impact or urgency is visible
- Prior actions are listed
- Evidence or reproduction clues are preserved
- The next owner knows what to do next

## Fail conditions
- The issue is vague
- Impact is missing
- The handoff lacks actions already taken
- Reproduction clues are absent
- The requested next step is unclear

## Minimum fix rule
Rewrite in this order:
1. Problem
2. Impact
3. Evidence
4. Actions already taken
5. Requested next action
