# Evidence Quality Gate

Use this gate before finalizing any comparison memo or recommendation.

## Pass conditions
- Evidence is separated from inference
- The decision question is explicit
- Trade-offs are visible
- Remaining uncertainty is acknowledged
- The recommendation is tied to evidence

## Fail conditions
- Claims are made without support
- The question being answered is unclear
- Trade-offs are hidden
- Confidence is overstated
- The memo reads like opinion without structure

## Minimum fix rule
Force this order:
1. Question
2. Evidence
3. Options
4. Trade-offs
5. Recommendation
6. Uncertainty
