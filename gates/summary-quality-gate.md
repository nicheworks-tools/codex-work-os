# Summary Quality Gate

Use this gate before finalizing any brief, digest, or summary.

## Pass conditions
- Facts and interpretation are clearly separated
- The reporting scope is clear
- Important risks are not buried
- A reader can understand the situation quickly
- Recommended next actions are included when relevant

## Fail conditions
- The summary is just compressed noise
- Progress is stated without impact
- Risks are missing or vague
- The output assumes context the reader may not have
- Next steps are absent despite operational need

## Minimum fix rule
If the summary fails, rewrite around:
1. What happened
2. What matters
3. What happens next
